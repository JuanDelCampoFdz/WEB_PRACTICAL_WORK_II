COMPUTER ENGINEERING + BUSINESS AND DATA ANALYTICS

PURPOSE

The aim of this degree is to combine two of the most promising fields, combining learning through real-world challenges and the integration of various disciplines (Law, Business, Economics, and Government) within the university. This program is designed to create interdisciplinary professionals with knowledge in business strategy, big data, software, and programming, training them to succeed in this digital revolution.

STRUCTURE

From the degree point of view, the Business and Data Analytics program is considered the primary degree, with Computer Engineering complementing it. As a result, the core courses in business and data analytics are concentrated in the early years, while the computer engineering courses are evenly distributed throughout the full 5.5-year duration of the program.

From the perspective of the academic years, the double degree spans 5.5 years in total. Each year includes courses from both degrees, except for the final half-year, which focuses solely on Computer Engineering subjects. Additionally, during the first semester of the first year, students take six fundamental courses, to gain a multidisciplinary knowledge, from UFV College as part of the Business and Data Analytics degree, since it is affiliated with the college.
