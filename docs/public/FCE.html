FUNDAMNENTALS OF COMPUTER ENGINEERING

DESCRIPTION

FCE is a course designed to provide an overview of computer engineering, as a theoretical field and as a practical way. In the theoretical aspects we can find facts such as the history of the discipline, information and data, computer components or HTML concepts. The course also focuses on practical skills, including teamwork, oral presentations on specific topics, and website development. In addition, FCE takes into account the importance of communication, collaboration, and technical abilities.
